# Associate Product Manager (Paid Internship)

## Role Overview
The Associate Product Manager (APM) Intern is an entry-level product management role designed for students or recent graduates passionate about building Quranic technology products. This paid internship provides hands-on experience in product management while contributing to features that serve millions of Muslims worldwide. APM interns work closely with Product Managers, designers, and engineers to deliver features that make Quranic knowledge more accessible.

## Key Responsibilities

### Product Development Support
- Assist Product Managers in defining feature requirements and user stories
- Create and maintain product documentation (PRDs, specifications)
- Support sprint planning and backlog prioritization
- Track feature development progress and coordinate with engineering teams
- Help identify and document product gaps and opportunities

### User Research & Analytics
- Conduct user interviews and gather feedback from Muslim community
- Analyze user behavior data using analytics platforms (Mixpanel, Amplitude)
- Document user pain points and feature requests
- Create user personas and journey maps
- Synthesize research findings into actionable product insights

### Cross-Functional Collaboration
- Participate in daily standups and sprint reviews
- Coordinate with designers on UI/UX improvements
- Work with Quranic content team on content-related features
- Support marketing team with feature launch communications
- Present findings and recommendations to stakeholders

### Market & Competitive Analysis
- Research competitor Quran apps and Islamic technology products
- Analyze market trends in Islamic EdTech and digital learning
- Document feature comparisons and industry best practices
- Identify opportunities for product differentiation
- Monitor user reviews and community feedback

## Required Skills

### Technical Skills
- Basic understanding of software development lifecycle
- Familiarity with product management tools (Jira, Linear, Notion, Asana)
- Proficiency with analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, or similar)
- Basic understanding of APIs and technical integrations
- Comfortable with data analysis and spreadsheets (Excel/Google Sheets)

### Product Management Fundamentals
- Understanding of Agile/Scrum methodologies
- User-centered design thinking approach
- Ability to write clear product specifications
- Basic wireframing and prototyping skills (Figma familiarity a plus)
- Prioritization frameworks (RICE, MoSCoW, etc.)

### Domain Knowledge
- Interest in Islamic content and Quranic learning
- Understanding of Muslim user needs and behaviors
- Awareness of Arabic language and RTL interface considerations
- Cultural sensitivity for religious content
- Passion for educational technology and digital learning

### Soft Skills
- Strong written and verbal communication in English
- Analytical thinking and problem-solving
- Collaborative mindset and team player attitude
- Self-motivated and proactive learner
- Time management and ability to handle multiple tasks
- Receptive to feedback and continuous improvement
