# Associate Product Manager Intern - ITQAN Community

## About ITQAN Community

ITQAN is on a mission to democratize access to Quranic knowledge through innovative, AI-native technology. We're building the next generation of Quranic learning tools that serve millions of Muslims worldwide—from students taking their first steps in understanding the Quran, to scholars conducting deep research into Islamic texts.

Our community brings together technologists, Islamic scholars, designers, and educators who share a passion for making the Quran more accessible, understandable, and actionable in daily life. We believe in the power of open collaboration, authentic scholarship, and cutting-edge technology to transform how people engage with the word of Allah.

At ITQAN, you'll work alongside a diverse, global team committed to excellence in both technology and Islamic authenticity, creating products that honor the sanctity of the Quran while leveraging modern innovation.

## The Opportunity

As an **Associate Product Manager (APM) Intern** at ITQAN, you'll launch your product management career while contributing to a mission that matters. This is not a "coffee-fetching" internship—you'll own real features, conduct meaningful user research, and make product decisions that impact millions of Muslims in their spiritual journey.

This **paid internship** offers hands-on experience working with senior product managers, engineers, and designers to build products that bridge technology and divine revelation. You'll learn product management fundamentals while contributing to the accessibility of Quranic knowledge worldwide.

**Location**: Remote (Egypt-based preferred)
**Duration**: 3-6 months
**Commitment**: 15-25 hours/week (part-time) or 35-40 hours/week (full-time)
**Compensation**: Competitive paid internship stipend

## What You'll Do

### Product Development (40% of time)
- **Own Features**: Take ownership of 1-2 product features from ideation to launch under mentor guidance
- **Write Specs**: Create product requirements documents (PRDs) and user stories for engineering teams
- **Sprint Planning**: Participate in Agile ceremonies (standups, sprint planning, retrospectives)
- **Track Progress**: Monitor feature development and coordinate with cross-functional teams
- **Make Trade-offs**: Learn to balance user needs, technical constraints, and business goals

### User Research & Insights (30% of time)
- **Interview Users**: Conduct 10+ user interviews with Muslim community members
- **Analyze Data**: Use analytics tools (Mixpanel, Google Analytics) to understand user behavior
- **Gather Feedback**: Monitor app reviews, community forums, and support tickets for insights
- **Create Personas**: Build user personas representing diverse Muslim learners
- **Present Findings**: Synthesize research into actionable recommendations for the team

### Cross-Functional Collaboration (20% of time)
- **Partner with Engineering**: Work closely with developers to clarify requirements and resolve questions
- **Coordinate with Design**: Collaborate with product designers on UX/UI solutions
- **Support Launches**: Help marketing team with feature announcement and user communication
- **Engage with Scholars**: Coordinate with Quranic content team on religiously sensitive features
- **Build Relationships**: Develop working relationships across all ITQAN teams

### Learning & Growth (10% of time)
- **Weekly Mentorship**: 1-on-1 sessions with Senior PM mentor for guidance and feedback
- **Product Training**: Access to product management courses, frameworks, and resources
- **Shadowing**: Observe senior PMs in strategic meetings and product reviews
- **Skill Building**: Develop expertise in tools like Figma, Jira, Notion, and analytics platforms
- **Career Development**: Build portfolio, refine resume, prepare for full-time PM roles
