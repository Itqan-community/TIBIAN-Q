# Product Strategy - ITQAN Community

## About ITQAN Community

ITQAN is on a mission to democratize access to Quranic knowledge through innovative, AI-native technology. We're building the next generation of Quranic learning tools that serve millions of Muslims worldwide—from students taking their first steps in understanding the Quran, to scholars conducting deep research into Islamic texts.

Our community brings together technologists, Islamic scholars, designers, and educators who share a passion for making the Quran more accessible, understandable, and actionable in daily life. We believe in the power of open collaboration, authentic scholarship, and cutting-edge technology to transform how people engage with the word of Allah.

At ITQAN, you'll work alongside a diverse, global team committed to excellence in both technology and Islamic authenticity, creating products that honor the sanctity of the Quran while leveraging modern innovation.

## The Opportunity

As **Product Strategy** lead at ITQAN, you'll define the long-term vision and strategic direction for our Quranic technology products. This is a high-impact role where you'll bridge business objectives with the spiritual needs of the Muslim ummah, creating comprehensive product roadmaps that shape how millions of people connect with the Quran in the digital age.

You'll work directly with executive leadership to identify strategic opportunities in the Quranic technology space, conduct market research, and develop 1-3 year roadmaps that balance short-term wins with transformational long-term vision. This is your chance to leave a lasting legacy in Islamic technology.

## What You'll Do

- **Define Strategic Vision**: Develop long-term product strategy aligned with ITQAN's mission to democratize Quranic knowledge; create comprehensive 1-3 year roadmaps that guide our product development
- **Lead Market Intelligence**: Conduct research into user needs across the global Muslim community, analyze competitor products in the Quranic app ecosystem, and identify gaps and opportunities in Islamic technology
- **Guide Prioritization**: Balance strategic value with user impact to prioritize features and initiatives; define success metrics and KPIs for strategic initiatives and quarterly planning frameworks
- **Drive Stakeholder Alignment**: Collaborate with executive leadership on strategic initiatives, present recommendations to stakeholders, and communicate product strategy clearly across the organization
- **Monitor Industry Trends**: Stay ahead of developments in Islamic technology, digital education, and AI applications to ensure ITQAN remains at the forefront of innovation

## What We're Looking For

- **Strategic Leadership**: 5+ years in product management or strategy roles with a proven track record of successful strategy execution; experience developing multi-year roadmaps and driving organizational alignment
- **Market & Data Analysis**: Strong proficiency with product strategy frameworks (OKRs, North Star metrics), market research methodologies, and data analysis to inform strategic decisions
- **Domain Expertise**: Understanding of Islamic scholarship and Quranic sciences; knowledge of digital Quran products and awareness of Muslim user demographics and needs (or deep eagerness to learn)
- **Executive Communication**: Exceptional presentation and communication skills; ability to articulate complex strategic concepts to diverse stakeholders including technical teams, scholars, and executives
- **Collaborative Mindset**: Cross-functional collaboration abilities, analytical problem-solving skills, and humble approach to leadership that values input from all team members

**Preferred**: MBA or advanced degree; experience in EdTech, religious technology, or mission-driven organizations serving Muslim audiences

## Why Join Us

- **Shape the Future**: Define the strategic direction for products that will impact how millions of Muslims engage with the Quran for generations to come
- **Intellectual Challenge**: Tackle unique strategic questions at the intersection of technology, education, and Islamic scholarship—work that few others in the world are doing
- **Executive Partnership**: Work directly with leadership team and have meaningful influence on company-wide strategy; clear path to Chief Product Officer, VP of Product, or Executive Leadership roles
- **Global Impact**: Your strategic decisions will serve a global community of 1.8+ billion Muslims, making Quranic knowledge more accessible across languages, cultures, and contexts
- **Mission & Growth**: Combine your strategic expertise with deep purpose; opportunity to expand from product strategy to company-wide strategic planning
