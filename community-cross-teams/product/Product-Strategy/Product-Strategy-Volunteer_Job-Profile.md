# Product Strategy Volunteer

## Role Overview
The Product Strategy Volunteer defines the long-term vision and strategic direction for ITQAN's Quranic technology products. This role bridges business objectives with the spiritual needs of the Muslim ummah, creating comprehensive product roadmaps that shape how millions of people connect with the Quran in the digital age.

## Key Responsibilities

### Strategic Planning & Vision (35% of time)
- Define product vision and long-term strategy aligned with ITQAN's mission
- Develop 1-3 year product roadmaps that guide product development
- Identify strategic opportunities in the Quranic technology space
- Balance short-term wins with transformational long-term vision
- Create quarterly and annual planning frameworks
- Define success metrics and KPIs for strategic initiatives

### Market Intelligence & Analysis (30% of time)
- Conduct research into user needs across the global Muslim community
- Analyze competitor products in the Quranic app ecosystem (Quran.com, Bayyinah, etc.)
- Identify gaps and opportunities in Islamic technology market
- Monitor Islamic EdTech and digital learning trends
- Study Muslim demographic and technology adoption trends
- Synthesize market insights into strategic recommendations

### Roadmap Development & Prioritization (20% of time)
- Prioritize features and initiatives based on strategic value and user impact
- Balance product-market fit with mission alignment
- Create dependency maps and implementation sequencing
- Define MVP scopes for strategic initiatives
- Align product roadmap with technical capabilities
- Guide product team on strategic priorities

### Stakeholder Collaboration (10% of time)
- Collaborate with executive leadership on strategic initiatives
- Present strategic recommendations to stakeholders
- Gather input from Product Managers, designers, and engineers
- Communicate product strategy clearly across organization
- Build consensus around strategic direction
- Facilitate strategic planning workshops

### Performance Monitoring (5% of time)
- Track progress against strategic goals and OKRs
- Monitor key product metrics and user growth
- Assess competitive positioning changes
- Conduct strategy review sessions
- Adjust roadmap based on learnings
