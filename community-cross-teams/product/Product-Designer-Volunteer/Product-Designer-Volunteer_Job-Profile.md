# Product Designer Volunteer

## Role Overview
The Product Designer Volunteer creates beautiful, intuitive, and accessible user experiences for ITQAN's Quranic technology products. This role designs interfaces that honor the sanctity of the Quran while providing modern, user-friendly experiences for millions of Muslims worldwide across web and mobile platforms.

## Key Responsibilities

### UI/UX Design (40% of time)
- Design user interfaces for web and mobile applications
- Create high-fidelity mockups and prototypes in Figma
- Design user flows and interaction patterns
- Ensure visual consistency across all platforms
- Design for both LTR and RTL (Arabic) interfaces
- Create responsive designs for different screen sizes

### Design Systems & Components (20% of time)
- Build and maintain design system components
- Create reusable UI patterns and components
- Document design guidelines and standards
- Ensure brand consistency across products
- Design icons, illustrations, and visual assets
- Maintain component library in Figma

### User-Centered Design (20% of time)
- Collaborate with User Research on user needs
- Create wireframes and low-fidelity prototypes
- Conduct design critiques and gather feedback
- Iterate designs based on user testing
- Design for accessibility and inclusivity
- Consider Islamic aesthetics and values

### Collaboration & Handoff (15% of time)
- Work closely with Product Managers on requirements
- Collaborate with engineers on design implementation
- Provide design specs and assets to developers
- Review implemented designs for quality
- Participate in sprint planning and reviews
- Communicate design decisions clearly

### Innovation & Exploration (5% of time)
- Explore new design trends and patterns
- Experiment with innovative interface concepts
- Research Islamic design principles and patterns
- Study competitor products for inspiration
- Prototype new ideas and concepts

## Required Skills

### Design Tools
- **Figma**: Advanced proficiency (primary design tool)
- **Adobe Creative Suite**: Photoshop, Illustrator (helpful)
- **Prototyping**: Interactive prototypes in Figma or Framer
- **Design Systems**: Component-based design
- **Handoff Tools**: Figma Dev Mode, Zeplin

### UI/UX Skills
- **Visual Design**: Typography, color theory, layout, hierarchy
- **Interaction Design**: Micro-interactions, animations, transitions
- **Information Architecture**: Organizing content logically
- **Wireframing**: Low-fidelity sketching and prototyping
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-first and adaptive layouts

### Technical Understanding
- **HTML/CSS Basics**: Understanding of web technologies
- **Design Tokens**: Variables, spacing systems, grids
- **Platform Guidelines**: iOS Human Interface, Material Design
- **Accessibility**: WCAG standards, inclusive design
- **Performance**: Designing for performance and optimization

### Domain Knowledge
- **Islamic Aesthetics**: Understanding of Islamic art and design
- **Arabic Typography**: Knowledge of Arabic type and calligraphy
- **RTL Design**: Right-to-left interface considerations
- **Quranic Content**: Respectful treatment of sacred text
- **Muslim Users**: Understanding of diverse Muslim users globally

### Soft Skills
- Strong visual and aesthetic sensibility
- Attention to detail and pixel perfection
- Collaborative and receptive to feedback
- Problem-solving and creative thinking
- Time management and organization
- Empathy for user needs
