# Product Manager Volunteer

## Role Overview
The Product Manager Volunteer is responsible for the end-to-end delivery of product features and initiatives at ITQAN. This role translates strategic vision into executable plans, working closely with engineers, designers, and Islamic scholars to build products that serve the Muslim ummah in their spiritual journey with the Quran.

## Key Responsibilities

### Feature Planning & Requirements (30% of time)
- Define feature requirements and detailed user stories
- Create product specifications and PRDs (Product Requirements Documents)
- Prioritize feature backlog based on user value and strategic alignment
- Break down large initiatives into deliverable increments
- Define acceptance criteria and success metrics
- Conduct feasibility analysis with engineering teams

### Development Collaboration (25% of time)
- Work closely with engineering teams on implementation
- Participate in sprint planning, standups, and retrospectives
- Make trade-off decisions on scope, timeline, and quality
- Unblock development team and resolve dependencies
- Review pull requests and provide product feedback
- Ensure features meet quality standards before launch

### User Research & Validation (20% of time)
- Conduct user research and gather feedback from Muslim community
- Test features with users before and after launch
- Define and monitor success metrics and KPIs
- Analyze user behavior and adoption patterns
- Identify pain points and improvement opportunities
- Validate assumptions through user testing

### Cross-Functional Coordination (15% of time)
- Coordinate with Product Designers on UX/UI
- Work with Marketing Team on feature launches
- Collaborate with Quranic Content Team on content features
- Communicate progress and blockers to stakeholders
- Facilitate cross-team alignment and decision-making
- Support customer support team with product knowledge

### Product Launch & Iteration (10% of time)
- Plan and execute feature launches
- Monitor post-launch adoption and user sentiment
- Gather feedback and iterate on features
- Document feature usage and lessons learned
- Prioritize bug fixes and improvements
- Communicate updates to users
