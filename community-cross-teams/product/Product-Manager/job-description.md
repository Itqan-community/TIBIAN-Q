# Product Manager - ITQAN Community

## About ITQAN Community

ITQAN is on a mission to democratize access to Quranic knowledge through innovative, AI-native technology. We're building the next generation of Quranic learning tools that serve millions of Muslims worldwide—from students taking their first steps in understanding the Quran, to scholars conducting deep research into Islamic texts.

Our community brings together technologists, Islamic scholars, designers, and educators who share a passion for making the Quran more accessible, understandable, and actionable in daily life. We believe in the power of open collaboration, authentic scholarship, and cutting-edge technology to transform how people engage with the word of Allah.

At ITQAN, you'll work alongside a diverse, global team committed to excellence in both technology and Islamic authenticity, creating products that honor the sanctity of the Quran while leveraging modern innovation.

## The Opportunity

As a **Product Manager** at ITQAN, you'll be at the intersection of technology and divine revelation—translating our vision of accessible Quranic knowledge into tangible product features that serve the Muslim ummah. You'll own the end-to-end delivery of features that impact millions of users in their spiritual journey, working closely with engineers, designers, and Islamic scholars to build products that are both technically excellent and spiritually meaningful.

This role offers the unique opportunity to shape how Muslims worldwide interact with the Quran in the digital age, making decisions that balance user needs, technical feasibility, and Islamic authenticity.

## What You'll Do

- **Drive Feature Delivery**: Define requirements, create detailed specifications, and prioritize the product backlog to deliver features that align with both user value and strategic goals
- **Collaborate Cross-Functionally**: Work closely with engineering teams on implementation, coordinate with designers on UX/UI, partner with Quranic scholars on content features, and support marketing on launches
- **Champion the User**: Conduct user research, gather feedback, define success metrics, and test features with real users to ensure we're building products that truly serve the Muslim community
- **Execute with Excellence**: Participate in sprint planning, make critical trade-off decisions, unblock development teams, and monitor post-launch adoption to continuously improve our products
- **Bridge Vision and Execution**: Translate strategic product vision into executable plans that deliver value incrementally while maintaining quality and Islamic authenticity

## What We're Looking For

- **Product Management Expertise**: 3+ years as a Product Manager with a proven track record of shipping digital products (mobile or web applications); proficiency with Agile/Scrum methodologies and product management tools
- **Technical Acumen**: Basic understanding of software development, APIs, and integrations; ability to work effectively with engineering teams and make informed technical trade-offs
- **Domain Knowledge**: Understanding of Islamic principles and values, respect for Quranic sanctity in product development, and familiarity with Arabic language and RTL interface considerations (or eagerness to learn)
- **Communication & Leadership**: Strong written and verbal communication skills, stakeholder management abilities, and empathy for user needs; comfort with ambiguity and fast-paced environments
- **Analytical Mindset**: Experience with analytics platforms (Mixpanel, Amplitude, etc.) and data-driven decision making to measure feature success and user satisfaction

**Preferred**: Experience in Islamic technology, EdTech, or mission-driven organizations

## Why Join Us

- **Meaningful Impact**: Your work will directly serve millions of Muslims in their journey to understand and connect with the Quran
- **Collaborative Excellence**: Work with Islamic scholars, world-class engineers, and passionate designers in an environment that values both technical innovation and spiritual authenticity
- **Growth & Learning**: Opportunity to deepen your understanding of Islamic scholarship while advancing your product management career; clear path to Senior PM, Principal PM, or Product Strategy roles
- **Flexible Environment**: Hybrid/remote work options with core collaboration times; we value outcomes over hours and trust our team to do their best work
- **Mission-Driven Culture**: Join a community united by the purpose of making Quranic knowledge accessible to all, where your faith and professional skills come together

If you're passionate about building products that serve a higher purpose and have the skills to deliver excellence at scale, we'd love to hear from you. Please submit your application through our community portal, including:

- Your resume highlighting relevant product management experience
- A brief cover letter explaining why you're excited about ITQAN's mission
