# Volunteer Coordinator

## Role Overview
The Volunteer Coordinator manages ITQAN's volunteer community, coordinating volunteer activities, tracking contributions, and ensuring volunteers have positive, meaningful experiences. This role serves as the central hub for volunteer engagement, recognition, and support.

## Key Responsibilities

### Volunteer Management (40% of time)
- Coordinate volunteer assignments and projects
- Match volunteers to appropriate roles
- Track volunteer hours and contributions
- Maintain volunteer database
- Communicate regularly with volunteers
- Ensure smooth volunteer experience

### Volunteer Engagement (30% of time)
- Onboard new volunteers
- Provide ongoing support and guidance
- Facilitate volunteer orientation
- Create volunteer community events
- Build volunteer network and connections
- Share volunteer success stories

### Recognition & Retention (20% of time)
- Recognize outstanding volunteer contributions
- Issue volunteer certificates
- Write recommendation letters
- Celebrate volunteer milestones
- Implement volunteer appreciation programs
- Track volunteer satisfaction

### Volunteer Operations (10% of time)
- Develop volunteer policies and guidelines
- Create volunteer handbook and materials
- Manage volunteer scheduling
- Coordinate volunteer training
- Report on volunteer metrics
- Improve volunteer processes

## Required Skills

### Volunteer Management
- Volunteer coordination
- Community building
- Project management
- Database management
- Relationship management

### Tools
- Volunteer management software
- Communication platforms (Slack, Discord)
- Scheduling tools (Calendly)
- Database management (Airtable, Notion)

### Soft Skills
- Highly organized
- Excellent communicator
- Warm and welcoming
- Appreciative and recognizing
- Detail-oriented

## Success Metrics
- Engagement: 80%+ active volunteer engagement
- Satisfaction: 4.5+/5 volunteer satisfaction
- Retention: 75%+ volunteer retention after 6 months
- Impact: Well-coordinated volunteer contributions

## Volunteer Engagement

### Time Commitment
- 15-20 hours per week
- Flexible remote work
- Regular volunteer coordination

### Duration
- 6-12 months initial commitment
- Renewable

### Benefits
- Build volunteer management expertise
- Create vibrant community
- Volunteer certificate
- Spiritual reward for coordinating Islamic tech volunteers

## Application Requirements
- Resume with volunteer management/coordination experience
- Cover letter on passion for community building
- Availability
