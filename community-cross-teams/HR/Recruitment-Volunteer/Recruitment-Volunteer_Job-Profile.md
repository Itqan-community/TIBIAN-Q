# Recruitment Volunteer

## Role Overview
The Recruitment Volunteer manages hiring and onboarding processes for ITQAN's growing community of volunteers, interns, and team members. This role identifies talent, conducts interviews, and ensures smooth onboarding experiences that align with ITQAN's mission and values.

## Key Responsibilities

### Talent Acquisition (40% of time)
- Source candidates through various channels (LinkedIn, job boards, referrals)
- Screen resumes and applications
- Conduct initial phone/video screening interviews
- Coordinate interview schedules with hiring teams
- Manage candidate communication and follow-ups
- Build talent pipeline for key roles

### Interview & Assessment (30% of time)
- Conduct structured interviews for volunteers and interns
- Assess cultural fit and alignment with mission
- Evaluate technical skills with hiring managers
- Check references and backgrounds
- Make hiring recommendations
- Provide candidate feedback

### Onboarding & Integration (20% of time)
- Create onboarding materials and checklists
- Conduct orientation sessions for new joiners
- Coordinate with teams for role-specific training
- Ensure smooth integration into teams
- Follow up with new members in first 90 days
- Gather onboarding feedback

### Employer Branding (10% of time)
- Promote ITQAN's volunteer opportunities
- Create compelling job descriptions
- Maintain careers page and recruitment materials
- Share success stories of volunteers
- Engage with Muslim tech communities
- Attend virtual recruitment events

## Required Skills

### Recruitment Expertise
- Sourcing and candidate screening
- Interviewing and assessment
- Applicant tracking systems (ATS)
- Job board management
- Reference checking

### Tools
- LinkedIn Recruiter
- Greenhouse, Lever, or similar ATS
- Google Workspace
- Video interview platforms (Zoom, Google Meet)

### Soft Skills
- Excellent communication
- People assessment
- Organization and time management
- Relationship building
- Cultural sensitivity

## Success Metrics
- Time-to-fill: <30 days for volunteer roles
- Candidate experience: 4.5+/5 satisfaction
- Hiring quality: 90%+ retention after 3 months
- Pipeline strength: 3-5 qualified candidates per open role

## Volunteer Engagement

### Time Commitment
- 10-20 hours per week
- Flexible remote work
- Higher during hiring sprints

### Duration
- 6-12 months initial commitment
- Renewable

### Benefits
- Build recruitment expertise
- Network with global talent
- Volunteer certificate
- Spiritual reward for building Islamic tech community

## Application Requirements
- Resume with recruitment/HR experience
- Cover letter on passion for talent acquisition
- Availability
