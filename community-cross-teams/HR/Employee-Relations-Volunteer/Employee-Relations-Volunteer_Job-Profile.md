# Employee Relations Volunteer

## Role Overview
The Employee Relations Volunteer nurtures a positive, inclusive, and supportive community culture at ITQAN. This role serves as a trusted point of contact for team members, addresses concerns, facilitates conflict resolution, and ensures everyone feels valued and supported in their contribution to the Quranic mission.

## Key Responsibilities

### Community Support (40% of time)
- Serve as point of contact for team member concerns
- Provide guidance and support to volunteers
- Conduct regular check-ins with team members
- Address interpersonal issues promptly
- Foster inclusive, respectful environment
- Support remote work wellbeing

### Culture Building (30% of time)
- Organize community events and celebrations
- Facilitate team bonding activities
- Promote ITQAN values and mission
- Recognize outstanding contributions
- Share success stories
- Build sense of belonging

### Conflict Resolution (20% of time)
- Mediate conflicts between team members
- Investigate concerns and complaints
- Provide fair, impartial guidance
- Facilitate difficult conversations
- Document and resolve issues
- Escalate when necessary

### Engagement & Retention (10% of time)
- Conduct engagement surveys
- Gather feedback on culture and experience
- Track volunteer satisfaction
- Identify retention risks
- Implement improvement initiatives
- Support volunteer recognition programs

## Required Skills

### HR & Relations
- Conflict resolution
- Active listening
- Emotional intelligence
- Mediation skills
- Confidentiality management

### Tools
- Survey tools (Google Forms, Typeform)
- Communication platforms (Slack, Discord)
- Documentation (Notion, Google Docs)

### Soft Skills
- Empathetic and compassionate
- Impartial and fair
- Trustworthy and discrete
- Patient and understanding
- Strong communicator

## Success Metrics
- Engagement: 80%+ team engagement scores
- Satisfaction: 4.5+/5 community satisfaction
- Retention: 85%+ volunteer retention rate
- Resolution: Issues resolved within 7 days

## Volunteer Engagement

### Time Commitment
- 10-15 hours per week
- Flexible remote work
- Responsive availability for urgent issues

### Duration
- 6-12 months initial commitment
- Renewable

### Benefits
- Develop HR and people skills
- Build supportive community
- Volunteer certificate
- Spiritual reward for supporting Muslim community

## Application Requirements
- Resume with HR/people experience
- Cover letter on passion for community building
- Availability
