# Training & Development Volunteer

## Role Overview
The Training & Development Volunteer designs and delivers learning programs that develop skills and capabilities across ITQAN's community. This role creates training materials, facilitates workshops, and ensures team members continuously grow in their technical and Islamic knowledge.

## Key Responsibilities

### Training Program Development (40% of time)
- Design training programs for volunteers and interns
- Create learning materials and documentation
- Develop onboarding training content
- Build skill development pathways
- Create Islamic EdTech domain training
- Design Arabic/RTL development training

### Training Delivery (30% of time)
- Facilitate workshops and training sessions
- Conduct technical skills training
- Lead lunch-and-learn sessions
- Mentor new volunteers
- Provide 1-on-1 coaching
- Host knowledge sharing sessions

### Learning Resources (20% of time)
- Curate online courses and resources
- Build internal knowledge base
- Create video tutorials and guides
- Maintain training library
- Share industry best practices
- Document tribal knowledge

### Performance & Growth (10% of time)
- Track skill development progress
- Gather training feedback
- Identify learning needs
- Support career development goals
- Measure training effectiveness
- Improve training programs

## Required Skills

### Learning & Development
- Instructional design
- Adult learning principles
- Training facilitation
- Curriculum development
- Performance improvement

### Tools
- LMS platforms (Moodle, Teachable)
- Video creation (Loom, Camtasia)
- Presentation software
- Knowledge management (Notion, Confluence)

### Soft Skills
- Excellent presentation skills
- Patient and supportive
- Organized and structured
- Empathetic and encouraging

## Success Metrics
- Training completion: 80%+ completion rates
- Satisfaction: 4.5+/5 training ratings
- Skill improvement: Measurable skill gains
- Knowledge retention: High retention rates

## Volunteer Engagement

### Time Commitment
- 10-20 hours per week
- Flexible remote work
- Some live training sessions

### Duration
- 6-12 months initial commitment
- Renewable

### Benefits
- Develop L&D expertise
- Impact community growth
- Volunteer certificate
- Spiritual reward for knowledge sharing

## Application Requirements
- Resume with training/L&D experience
- Cover letter on passion for learning and development
- Sample training materials (optional)
- Availability
