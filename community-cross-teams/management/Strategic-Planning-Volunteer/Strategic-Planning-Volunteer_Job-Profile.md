# Strategic Planning Volunteer

## Role Overview
The Strategic Planning Volunteer shapes ITQAN's long-term vision and strategy, ensuring the organization effectively pursues its mission to democratize Quranic knowledge. This role conducts strategic analysis, develops multi-year roadmaps, identifies growth opportunities, and ensures strategic alignment across all teams.

## Key Responsibilities

### Strategic Planning & Roadmapping (35% of time)
- Develop annual and multi-year strategic plans for ITQAN
- Define organizational goals, objectives, and key results (OKRs)
- Create strategic roadmaps across product, technology, and community
- Align strategic initiatives with mission and vision
- Identify strategic priorities and trade-offs
- Translate strategy into actionable plans for execution teams

### Market & Competitive Analysis (25% of time)
- Research Quranic technology landscape and trends
- Analyze competitor products and strategies (Quran.com, Bayyinah, etc.)
- Identify market opportunities and underserved user segments
- Monitor Islamic EdTech and digital learning trends
- Study Muslim demographic and technology adoption trends
- Synthesize insights into strategic recommendations

### Partnership & Growth Strategy (20% of time)
- Identify strategic partnership opportunities (Islamic organizations, mosques, schools)
- Develop business development strategies
- Research grant and funding opportunities for Islamic nonprofits
- Explore monetization strategies aligned with mission
- Plan geographic expansion and localization strategies
- Design community growth and engagement strategies

### Performance Tracking & Analysis (10% of time)
- Monitor progress against strategic goals and OKRs
- Track key performance indicators (KPIs) for the organization
- Analyze organizational health metrics
- Generate strategic dashboards and reports for leadership
- Identify strategic risks and mitigation strategies
- Conduct post-mortems on strategic initiatives

### Stakeholder Communication (10% of time)
- Present strategic plans to leadership and teams
- Facilitate strategic planning sessions and workshops
- Communicate strategic direction across organization
- Gather input from teams on strategic opportunities
- Build alignment around strategic priorities
