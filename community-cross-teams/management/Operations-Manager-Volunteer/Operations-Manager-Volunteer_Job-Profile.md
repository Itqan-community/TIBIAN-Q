# Operations Manager Volunteer

## Role Overview
The Operations Manager Volunteer oversees the day-to-day operations of ITQAN Community, ensuring smooth execution across all teams (product, development, marketing, content). This role optimizes processes, coordinates cross-functional initiatives, manages operational workflows, and ensures the organization runs efficiently to serve millions of Muslims worldwide.

## Key Responsibilities

### Operations Oversight & Coordination (30% of time)
- Oversee daily operations across product, engineering, marketing, and content teams
- Coordinate cross-functional projects and initiatives
- Ensure alignment between teams on timelines and deliverables
- Identify and resolve operational bottlenecks
- Facilitate communication and information flow between teams
- Monitor organizational health and team productivity

### Process Optimization & Documentation (25% of time)
- Analyze existing workflows and identify improvement opportunities
- Design and implement efficient operational processes
- Document standard operating procedures (SOPs)
- Create templates and checklists for recurring tasks
- Implement process automation using tools like Zapier, Make, or Notion
- Continuously iterate processes based on feedback and results

### Project & Resource Management (20% of time)
- Track major projects and initiatives across organization
- Manage resource allocation and workload distribution
- Monitor project timelines and milestone completion
- Coordinate between teams to resolve dependencies
- Maintain organizational calendar of deadlines and events
- Ensure projects stay on track and within scope

### Tools & Systems Management (15% of time)
- Manage operational tools (Notion, Slack, project management tools)
- Maintain organizational wikis and knowledge bases
- Implement and optimize collaboration tools
- Train team members on operational systems
- Evaluate new tools and technologies for efficiency
- Ensure data security and access control

### Reporting & Analytics (10% of time)
- Generate operational reports and dashboards
- Track key operational metrics (velocity, cycle time, utilization)
- Analyze team performance and productivity trends
- Present insights to leadership for decision-making
- Monitor budget utilization and resource efficiency
- Identify data-driven improvement opportunities
