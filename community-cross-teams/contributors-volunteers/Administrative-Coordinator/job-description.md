# Administrative Coordinator - ITQAN Community

## About ITQAN Community

ITQAN is on a mission to democratize access to Quranic knowledge through innovative, AI-native technology. We're building the next generation of Quranic learning tools that serve millions of Muslims worldwide—from students taking their first steps in understanding the Quran, to scholars conducting deep research into Islamic texts.

Our community brings together technologists, Islamic scholars, designers, and educators who share a passion for making the Quran more accessible, understandable, and actionable in daily life.

## The Opportunity

Join ITQAN as an **Administrative Coordinator** and become the organizational backbone that enables our teams to build world-class Quranic technology. This role is perfect for highly organized, detail-oriented individuals who love creating order, coordinating complex schedules, and ensuring everything runs smoothly.

Whether you're looking for a **paid internship** to launch your career in operations and project management, or seeking a **meaningful volunteer opportunity** to contribute your organizational skills while building experience—this role offers both pathways.

**Location**: Remote (Egypt-based preferred, global applicants welcome)
**Engagement Options**:
- **Paid Internship**: 20-30 hours/week, 3-6 months, competitive stipend
- **Volunteer**: 10-20 hours/week, flexible duration, unpaid

## What You'll Do

Every day, you'll ensure ITQAN runs like a well-oiled machine:

### Meeting Management (30% of time)
- **Schedule Smart**: Coordinate 15-20 meetings per week across global teams and time zones
- **Run Meetings**: Prepare agendas, take detailed notes, track action items
- **Follow Through**: Ensure meeting decisions turn into action with systematic follow-ups
- **Virtual Hosting**: Manage Zoom/Google Meet logistics for webinars and all-hands meetings

### Presentation & Documentation (25% of time)
- **Create Impact**: Design professional presentations for leadership, partners, and community
- **Visualize Data**: Transform complex information into clear, compelling slides
- **Polish Deliverables**: Format reports, proposals, and stakeholder communications
- **Maintain Templates**: Keep brand-consistent presentation and document templates

### File Organization (20% of time)
- **Organize Everything**: Maintain crystal-clear file structures in Google Drive and Notion
- **Version Control**: Manage document versions and archiving
- **Easy Access**: Ensure anyone can find what they need in seconds
- **Audit & Clean**: Regular file audits to remove clutter and maintain organization

### Project Coordination (15% of time)
- **Track Progress**: Update project management tools with milestones and deadlines
- **Coordinate Handoffs**: Ensure smooth transitions between teams
- **Status Reporting**: Compile progress updates for leadership
- **Support Orchestration**: Assist Orchestration Managers with administrative project needs

### Team Operations (10% of time)
- **Onboarding Support**: Help new volunteers and interns get set up
- **Event Coordination**: Organize virtual team events and community gatherings
- **Communication Hub**: Manage team calendars and announcements
- **Process Documentation**: Create and maintain standard operating procedures

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need prior administrative experience?**
A: For **paid internships**, 1-2 years of relevant experience is preferred. For **volunteer positions**, no experience required—just strong organizational skills and willingness to learn.

**Q: What's the difference between paid internship and volunteer tracks?**
A: **Paid internships** require 20-30 hours/week, offer competitive stipend, and run 3-6 months with career development focus. **Volunteers** commit 10-20 hours/week on flexible schedules, unpaid, with focus on skill-building and contribution.

**Q: Can I start as a volunteer and transition to paid internship?**
A: Absolutely! Many volunteers who demonstrate excellence and capacity are offered paid internship or even permanent roles.

**Q: What tools will I use?**
A: Primarily Google Workspace (Drive, Docs, Sheets, Slides, Calendar), Notion or Asana for project management, Slack for communication, and Canva for presentations. Training provided!

**Q: Do I need to be in Egypt?**
A: Egypt-based candidates preferred for time zone alignment, but global applicants are welcome. The role is 100% remote.

**Q: Do I need to be Muslim?**
A: No, but you should respect Islamic values and be genuinely passionate about ITQAN's mission to serve the Muslim community.

**Q: What schedule flexibility do I have?**
A: Very flexible! Work when it suits your schedule, with some core overlap hours (typically 10 AM - 2 PM Egypt time) for meetings and collaboration.

**Q: Can I apply while studying?**
A: Yes! Many of our Administrative Coordinators are students balancing studies with part-time work or volunteering.

**Q: What opportunities for growth exist?**
A: You can progress to Project Coordinator, Operations Manager, or Executive Assistant roles. We prioritize internal promotions and career development.
