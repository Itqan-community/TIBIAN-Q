# Administrative Coordinator

## Role Overview
The Administrative Coordinator is the organizational backbone of ITQAN Community operations, providing critical support in project coordination, meeting management, presentation preparation, file organization, and administrative tasks. This role can be filled as either a **paid internship** or **volunteer position**, offering flexible engagement while ensuring smooth operations across all teams.

This role is ideal for highly organized individuals who excel at multitasking, love creating order from chaos, and want to contribute to Quranic technology while developing professional administrative and project coordination skills.

## Key Responsibilities

### Meeting Management & Coordination
- Schedule and coordinate meetings across multiple teams and time zones
- Send meeting invitations with agendas and required materials
- Prepare meeting agendas in collaboration with meeting owners
- Take detailed meeting notes and distribute minutes to attendees
- Track action items and follow up on commitments
- Manage recurring team meetings (standups, sprint reviews, all-hands)
- Coordinate with external partners for meeting scheduling

### Presentation Preparation & Support
- Create professional presentations using Google Slides, PowerPoint, or Canva
- Design visually appealing slides with brand consistency
- Compile data, charts, and visuals for leadership presentations
- Format and polish presentations for stakeholder meetings
- Prepare speaker notes and talking points
- Convert complex information into clear, visual formats
- Maintain presentation template library

### File & Document Management
- Organize digital files using consistent naming conventions
- Maintain organized folder structures in Google Drive/Dropbox
- Manage document version control and archiving
- Create and maintain documentation repositories
- Ensure team members can easily find resources
- Implement and enforce file organization standards
- Regularly audit and clean up outdated files

### Project Coordination Support
- Track project milestones and deadlines
- Update project management tools (Notion, Asana, ClickUp)
- Coordinate handoffs between teams
- Monitor task completion and send reminders
- Prepare status reports and progress updates
- Maintain project documentation and timelines
- Support orchestration managers with administrative tasks

### Administrative Operations
- Manage shared team calendars
- Coordinate team events and virtual gatherings
- Handle email correspondence and communications
- Maintain contact lists and directories
- Process reimbursements and expense documentation
- Support onboarding of new team members
- Create and update standard operating procedures (SOPs)

### Communication & Liaison
- Serve as communication hub for administrative requests
- Coordinate between different teams for information gathering
- Draft professional emails and announcements
- Translate meeting decisions into clear action plans
- Support partner communication and coordination
- Facilitate information flow across teams

- **Commitment**: 10-20 hours per week
