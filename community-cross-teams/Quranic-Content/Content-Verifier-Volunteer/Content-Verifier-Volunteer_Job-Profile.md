# Content Verifier Volunteer

## Role Overview
The Content Verifier Volunteer ensures the accuracy and authenticity of all Quranic content in ITQAN's products. This role meticulously verifies Quranic text, verse references, audio recitations, and related content to maintain the highest standards of accuracy for this sacred material.

## Key Responsibilities

### Quranic Text Verification (40% of time)
- Verify Quranic text accuracy character by character
- Check diacritical marks (tashkeel, harakat) correctness
- Validate Arabic spelling and orthography
- Ensure proper Quranic script standards
- Cross-reference with mushaf (printed Quran)
- Verify verse boundaries and numbering

### Reference Validation (25% of time)
- Validate surah and ayah numbering
- Check verse citations and references
- Verify cross-references between verses
- Validate chapter names and information
- Ensure accurate revelation order
- Check Makki/Madani classifications

### Audio & Multimedia Verification (20% of time)
- Verify audio recitations match text
- Check recitation accuracy and tajweed
- Validate reciter attributions
- Ensure audio-text synchronization
- Review multimedia content for accuracy
- Verify Arabic pronunciation in audio

### Database & Metadata Quality (15% of time)
- Verify database content integrity
- Check metadata accuracy
- Validate tags and classifications
- Ensure data consistency
- Review import/export accuracy
- Monitor content updates

## Required Skills
