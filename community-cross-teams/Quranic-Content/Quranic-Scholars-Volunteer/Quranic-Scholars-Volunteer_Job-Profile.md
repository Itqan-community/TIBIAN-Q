# Quranic Scholars Volunteer

## Role Overview
The Quranic Scholars Volunteer provides Islamic scholarly expertise and guidance to ensure ITQAN's products maintain the highest standards of Islamic authenticity and respect for the Quran. This role reviews content, validates interpretations, and ensures all Quranic material honors the sanctity of Allah's word.

## Key Responsibilities

### Scholarly Review & Validation (50% of time)
- Review Quranic content for Islamic accuracy
- Validate translations and interpretations
- Ensure proper treatment of sacred text
- Review tafsir (commentary) content
- Validate Arabic linguistic accuracy
- Provide Islamic scholarly guidance

### Content Guidance (25% of time)
- Guide product team on Islamic principles
- Advise on Quranic presentation standards
- Review feature concepts for Islamic appropriateness
- Provide scholarly opinions on content questions
- Ensure respect for sacred text in UI/UX
- Advise on cultural and religious sensitivities

### Quality Assurance (15% of time)
- Verify Quranic verse accuracy
- Check diacritical marks (tashkeel) correctness
- Review Arabic text rendering
- Validate verse numbering and references
- Ensure tajweed rules are respected
- Check Islamic terminology usage

### Education & Training (10% of time)
- Educate team on Islamic scholarship
- Provide Quranic knowledge training
- Share Islamic perspectives on features
- Create guidelines for content handling
- Build team's Islamic literacy
- Foster respect for sacred content

## Required Skills

### Islamic Scholarship
- Deep knowledge of Quran and tafsir
- Understanding of Classical Arabic
- Familiarity with Quranic sciences (Ulum al-Quran)
- Knowledge of hadith and Islamic jurisprudence
- Awareness of multiple scholarly traditions

### Content Review
- Textual analysis and verification
- Attention to detail
- Critical thinking
- Research skills
- Cross-reference validation

### Communication
- Ability to explain complex Islamic concepts simply
- Patient and educational approach
- Respectful collaboration with non-scholars
- Clear written communication

## Qualifications

### Islamic Education
- Formal Islamic education (preferred)
- Ijazah in Quran or Islamic studies (plus)
- Deep self-study of Quran and tafsir
- Knowledge of multiple tafsir works

### Experience
- Background in Islamic education or scholarship
- Experience teaching Quran or Islamic studies
- Content review or editorial experience (helpful)

### Cultural Fit
- Deep commitment to Islamic values
- Respect for diverse Islamic traditions
- Patient and humble in sharing knowledge
- Mission-aligned with democratizing Quranic access

## Success Metrics
- Accuracy: 100% Islamic accuracy in reviewed content
- Review quality: Thorough, detailed scholarly feedback
- Team education: Improved team Islamic literacy
- Responsiveness: Timely reviews (48-72 hours)

## Volunteer Engagement

### Time Commitment
- 10-15 hours per week
- Flexible remote work
- Responsive for urgent scholarly questions

### Duration
- 12 months initial commitment (continuity important)
- Renewable

### Benefits
- Serve Allah's book and Muslim ummah
- Impact millions of Muslims' Quran engagement
- Network with scholars globally
- Volunteer certificate
- Spiritual reward (sadaqah jariyah) for facilitating Quranic knowledge

- Cover letter on Islamic scholarship and passion for Quranic technology
