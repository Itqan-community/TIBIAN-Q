# Translation Reviewer Volunteer

## Role Overview
The Translation Reviewer Volunteer ensures the quality, accuracy, and Islamic appropriateness of Quranic translations across multiple languages. This role reviews translations, compares them with original Arabic meanings, and ensures translations serve diverse Muslim communities while maintaining fidelity to the sacred text.

## Key Responsibilities

### Translation Review (50% of time)
- Review Quranic translations for accuracy
- Compare translations with Arabic original
- Verify linguistic quality and fluency
- Check consistency across verses
- Identify translation errors or ambiguities
- Validate multiple translations for same verses

### Quality Assurance (25% of time)
- Ensure respect for sacred text in translation
- Verify transliteration accuracy
- Check footnotes and explanatory notes
- Validate cultural appropriateness
- Ensure gender-sensitive language where appropriate
- Review formatting and presentation

### Cross-Reference Validation (15% of time)
- Compare with established scholarly translations
- Cross-reference multiple tafsir sources
- Validate against Arabic linguistic meanings
- Check consistency with Islamic tradition
- Ensure accuracy of technical Islamic terms
- Verify proper nouns and names

### Documentation & Feedback (10% of time)
- Document translation issues and corrections
- Provide feedback to content team
- Create translation guidelines
- Report on translation quality metrics
- Suggest improvements to translation workflow
- Build translation quality database

## Required Skills

### Linguistic Expertise
- Proficiency in Arabic and target language(s)
- Understanding of Classical Arabic
- Translation theory and principles
- Comparative linguistics
- Cultural translation sensitivity

### Quranic Knowledge
- Understanding of Quranic meanings and context
- Familiarity with major Quran translations
- Knowledge of tafsir literature
- Awareness of translation challenges in Quranic text

### Review Skills
- Attention to detail
- Critical analysis
- Comparative evaluation
- Quality assessment
- Documentation skills

## Qualifications

### Language Skills
- Native or near-native proficiency in target language
- Strong Arabic reading comprehension
- Translation or interpretation experience
- Bilingual background (preferred)

### Islamic Knowledge
- Understanding of Quranic concepts and terminology
- Familiarity with Islamic tradition
- Knowledge of Quranic translation history

### Experience
- Background in translation or linguistics
- Experience reviewing religious or sensitive texts
- Editorial or proofreading experience (helpful)

## Success Metrics
- Translation accuracy: 99%+ accuracy in reviewed translations
- Review coverage: Comprehensive review of all translations
- Issue identification: Clear, actionable feedback
- Consistency: Uniform quality across translations
