# Tafsir Curator Volunteer

## Role Overview
The Tafsir Curator Volunteer curates, organizes, and presents Quranic commentary (tafsir) from classical and contemporary scholars. This role makes tafsir accessible to diverse Muslim audiences, ensuring high-quality interpretative content that enriches understanding of the Quran while maintaining scholarly standards.

## Key Responsibilities

### Tafsir Content Curation (40% of time)
- Research and select tafsir from reputable scholars
- Curate commentary from multiple schools of thought
- Organize tafsir by verse, theme, and scholar
- Summarize lengthy tafsir for accessibility
- Select relevant portions for specific contexts
- Balance classical and contemporary tafsir

### Content Organization (25% of time)
- Structure tafsir content logically
- Create thematic tafsir collections
- Organize by scholarly tradition and methodology
- Tag and categorize commentary
- Build searchable tafsir database
- Create user-friendly tafsir navigation

### Quality & Authenticity (20% of time)
- Verify tafsir attributions to scholars
- Ensure scholarly credibility
- Review for Islamic accuracy
- Validate sources and citations
- Check translations of tafsir (when applicable)
- Maintain scholarly standards

### User Experience Design (15% of time)
- Present tafsir in accessible formats
- Create layered explanations (beginner to advanced)
- Design contextual tafsir experiences
- Ensure mobile-friendly formatting
- Support different user learning styles
- Gather feedback on tafsir presentation

## Required Skills

### Islamic Scholarship
- Deep knowledge of tafsir literature
- Familiarity with major classical and contemporary tafsir works
- Understanding of usul al-tafsir (principles of interpretation)
- Awareness of different tafsir methodologies
- Knowledge of scholarly traditions

### Tafsir Works Knowledge
- Classical: Ibn Kathir, Tabari, Qurtubi, Ibn Ashur
- Contemporary: Sayyid Qutb, Maududi, Sha'rawi
- Linguistic: Zamakhshari, Razi
- Thematic and topical tafsir

### Curation Skills
- Content research and selection
- Editorial judgment
- Organization and categorization
- Summarization and synthesis
- User-centered content design

### Technical Skills
- Content management systems
- Database organization
- Tagging and metadata
- Digital content formatting

## Qualifications

### Islamic Education
- Formal or informal Islamic education
- Deep study of tafsir literature
- Understanding of Quranic sciences

### Experience
- Background in Islamic education or scholarship

### Cultural Fit

- Balance of depth and accessibility
- Commitment to diverse Islamic perspectives
