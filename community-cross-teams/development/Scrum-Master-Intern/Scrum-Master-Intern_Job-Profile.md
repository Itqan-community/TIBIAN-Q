# Scrum Master Intern

## Role Overview
The Scrum Master Intern facilitates Agile practices and supports development teams in delivering high-quality Quranic technology products efficiently. This role focuses on learning and applying Scrum methodology, facilitating ceremonies, removing impediments, and fostering continuous improvement within engineering teams.

This position can be engaged as either a **paid internship** or **volunteer opportunity**, providing hands-on experience in Agile project management while contributing to products that serve millions of Muslims worldwide.

## Key Responsibilities

### Scrum Ceremony Facilitation (35% of time)
- **Daily Standups**: Facilitate 15-minute daily standups, ensure team alignment and blocker identification
- **Sprint Planning**: Support sprint planning sessions, help team commit to realistic goals
- **Sprint Reviews**: Organize sprint demos, coordinate stakeholder attendance and feedback
- **Sprint Retrospectives**: Facilitate team retrospectives, capture action items for improvement
- **Backlog Refinement**: Assist in story estimation, acceptance criteria clarification, and backlog grooming
- **Meeting Time Management**: Ensure ceremonies are time-boxed and productive

### Team Support & Impediment Removal (25% of time)
- **Blocker Resolution**: Identify and help remove obstacles blocking team progress
- **Communication Facilitation**: Bridge communication between developers, product managers, and stakeholders
- **Resource Coordination**: Help team access needed resources, tools, or information
- **Conflict Mediation**: Address team conflicts or miscommunications respectfully
- **Cross-Team Coordination**: Facilitate dependencies and handoffs between teams
- **Proactive Problem-Solving**: Anticipate potential blockers and address them early

### Process Improvement & Metrics (20% of time)
- **Track Sprint Metrics**: Monitor velocity, burndown charts, and sprint completion rates
- **Identify Patterns**: Spot trends in team performance and bottlenecks
- **Process Documentation**: Document team workflows and Agile practices
- **Continuous Improvement**: Implement action items from retrospectives
- **Best Practice Research**: Study Agile methodologies and suggest improvements
- **Tool Optimization**: Optimize use of project management tools (Jira, Linear, Notion)

### Scrum Board & Documentation Management (15% of time)
- **Board Maintenance**: Keep Scrum boards updated with accurate task status
- **Sprint Documentation**: Create sprint summaries, retro notes, and action item tracking
- **Metrics Reporting**: Generate sprint reports and team performance dashboards
- **Team Calendar**: Manage team calendar with all ceremonies and important dates
- **Knowledge Base**: Maintain Agile process documentation for team reference

### Learning & Development (5% of time)
- **Agile Study**: Learn Scrum framework, Agile principles, and servant leadership
- **Certification Preparation**: Study for Scrum certifications (CSM, PSM)
- **Mentorship**: Shadow experienced Scrum Masters and learn from their practices
- **Community Engagement**: Participate in Agile communities and learning groups
- **Skill Building**: Develop facilitation, coaching, and leadership skills
