## About ITQAN Community

ITQAN is on a mission to democratize access to Quranic knowledge through innovative, AI-native technology. We're building the next generation of Quranic learning tools that serve millions of Muslims worldwide—from students taking their first steps in understanding the Quran, to scholars conducting deep research into Islamic texts.

Our products combine beautiful design, cutting-edge technology, and Islamic authenticity to create transformative learning experiences. Every line of code you write helps Muslims connect with the word of Allah.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Which specialization should I choose?**
A: Choose based on your interests and skills. **Frontend** if you love UI/UX and visual work. **Backend** if you enjoy data, APIs, and system architecture. **Mobile** if you want to build native apps. **Full-Stack** if you want to do it all.

**Q: Do I need prior internship experience?**
A: For **paid internships**, a portfolio with 2-3 projects is required (can be academic or personal). For **volunteers**, just foundational programming knowledge.

**Q: What programming languages should I know?**
A: For **Frontend/Mobile**: JavaScript/TypeScript. For **Backend**: JavaScript/TypeScript, Python, or similar. For **Full-Stack**: Both frontend and backend languages.

**Q: Can I switch specializations during the internship?**
A: Yes! While you'll start in one area, there's flexibility to explore and work across specializations as you grow.

**Q: Do I need to know Arabic?**
A: Not required! Understanding of RTL interfaces is helpful but can be learned on the job.

**Q: Will I actually ship code to production?**
A: Absolutely! Most interns ship 2-4 features to production during their internship. Real impact, real users.

**Q: Can I apply while finishing my degree?**
A: Yes! Many of our interns are students balancing coursework with internships.

**Q: What's the difference between paid and volunteer tracks?**
A: **Paid**: Requires portfolio, offers stipend, 20-30 hrs/week, career focus. **Volunteer**: Foundational skills needed, unpaid, 10-20 hrs/week, learning focus.

**Q: Can volunteers become paid interns or full-time?**
A: Absolutely! Volunteers who build strong portfolios and demonstrate excellence are prioritized for paid positions.

**Q: Do I need to be Muslim?**
A: No, but you should respect Islamic values and be genuinely passionate about serving the Muslim community.
