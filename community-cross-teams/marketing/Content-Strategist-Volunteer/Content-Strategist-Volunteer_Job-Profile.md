# Content Strategist Volunteer (AI-Driven)

## Role Overview
The Content Strategist Volunteer develops and executes ITQAN's content vision using AI-powered workflows to create, distribute, and optimize content that serves the Muslim community. This role combines strategic content planning with AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Jasper, etc.) to scale content production while maintaining Islamic authenticity and quality.

## Key Responsibilities

### Content Strategy & Planning (30% of time)
- Develop monthly/quarterly content strategies aligned with ITQAN's mission
- Create editorial calendars coordinating blog posts, social media, videos, emails
- Identify content themes resonating with Muslim audiences (Ramadan, Hajj, daily Islamic living)
- Research content gaps and opportunities in Quranic education space
- Define content pillars and messaging frameworks
- Align content strategy with product launches and community initiatives

### AI-Driven Content Creation (25% of time)
- Use AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Jasper) to generate content drafts
- Create prompt templates for consistent, high-quality AI output
- Edit and refine AI-generated content for Islamic accuracy and authenticity
- Develop AI workflows that scale content production 10x
- Train team members on effective AI prompting for content
- Ensure AI content respects Quranic sanctity and Islamic scholarship

### Content Distribution & Optimization (20% of time)
- Optimize content for SEO (keywords, meta descriptions, structure)
- Coordinate content distribution across channels (blog, social, email, partners)
- Repurpose content for different formats (blog → social → video → email)
- Build content distribution workflows and automation
- Track content performance across channels
- Optimize content based on engagement data

### Team Collaboration & Coordination (15% of time)
- Brief writers, designers, and video editors on content needs
- Review and approve content for quality and Islamic accuracy
- Coordinate with product team on feature content
- Work with Quranic scholars to ensure theological accuracy
- Manage content approval workflows
- Facilitate content planning meetings

### Analytics & Continuous Improvement (10% of time)
- Track content metrics (traffic, engagement, conversions, shares)
- Analyze which content types perform best
- Identify trending topics in Muslim community
- Test different content formats and approaches
- Generate content performance reports
- Iterate strategy based on data insights
