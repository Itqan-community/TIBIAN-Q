# Digital Marketing Volunteer

## Role Overview
The Digital Marketing Volunteer drives online visibility and user acquisition for ITQAN's Quranic technology products through digital marketing campaigns, SEO optimization, paid advertising, and data analytics. This volunteer role offers hands-on experience building marketing campaigns that reach millions of Muslims worldwide while contributing to accessible Quranic knowledge.

## Key Responsibilities

### Campaign Management (30% of time)
- Plan and execute digital marketing campaigns across channels (Google Ads, Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn)
- Develop campaign strategies aligned with product launches and community goals
- Create ad copy and creative briefs for campaign assets
- Monitor campaign performance and optimize for conversions
- Coordinate with content and design teams for campaign materials
- A/B test different messaging, audiences, and creative approaches

### SEO & Content Optimization (25% of time)
- Conduct keyword research for Quranic and Islamic education content
- Optimize website content for search engines (on-page SEO)
- Build backlink strategies and outreach for domain authority
- Monitor SEO performance using Google Analytics and Search Console
- Optimize meta descriptions, titles, and structured data
- Research competitor SEO strategies and identify opportunities

### Paid Advertising (20% of time)
- Set up and manage Google Ads campaigns (Search, Display, YouTube)
- Create and optimize Facebook/Instagram ad campaigns
- Manage ad budgets and bid strategies for ROI optimization
- Analyze conversion funnels and improve landing pages
- Test different ad formats and targeting strategies
- Report on ad performance and budget utilization

### Analytics & Reporting (15% of time)
- Track website traffic, user behavior, and conversion metrics
- Create dashboards using Google Analytics, Data Studio, or similar
- Generate weekly/monthly marketing performance reports
- Identify trends and insights from data to inform strategy
- Measure ROI of marketing campaigns and channels
- Provide data-driven recommendations for optimization

### Growth & Experimentation (10% of time)
- Test new marketing channels and tactics
- Research emerging digital marketing trends
- Experiment with growth hacking strategies
- Analyze user acquisition funnels and identify drop-off points
- Propose innovative campaigns to reach Muslim audiences globally

## Required Skills

### Digital Marketing Fundamentals
- Understanding of digital marketing channels (SEO, SEM, social ads, email)
- Knowledge of marketing funnels and customer journey
- Familiarity with Google Ads and Facebook Ads platforms
- Basic understanding of conversion optimization
- Awareness of marketing metrics (CTR, CPA, CAC, ROAS, LTV)

### Technical Skills
- **Google Analytics**: Traffic analysis and conversion tracking

- **Facebook Ads Manager**: Campaign creation and optimization
- **SEO Tools**: Google Search Console, SEMrush, Ahrefs, or similar
- **Spreadsheets**: Data analysis with Excel or Google Sheets

### Analytical & Strategic Thinking
- Data-driven decision making
- Ability to analyze campaign performance and identify improvements
- Strategic thinking about audience targeting and positioning
- Understanding of A/B testing and experimentation

### Content & Copywriting

- Understanding of persuasive messaging for Muslim audiences

### Soft Skills
