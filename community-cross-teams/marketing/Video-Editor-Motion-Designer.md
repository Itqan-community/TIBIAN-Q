# Video Editor / Motion Designer (AI-Enhanced)

## Role Overview
The Video Editor / Motion Designer creates compelling video content for ITQAN Community using hybrid workflows that combine professional editing tools (DaVinci Resolve) with cutting-edge AI video technologies (Runway, Pika). This role is integral to visual content production, bringing Quranic technology stories to life through motion graphics, storytelling, and AI-enhanced editing techniques.

**Mission**: Produce high-quality, engaging video content that showcases ITQAN products and Islamic values while leveraging AI tools to maximize efficiency and creative possibilities.

## Key Responsibilities

### Video Production & Editing
- Edit video content for social media, YouTube, marketing campaigns, and product demos
- Create motion graphics and animations for explainer videos and promotional content
- Color grade and finalize videos to professional broadcast standards
- Edit podcast content and video interviews
- Produce short-form content optimized for TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts
- Maintain brand consistency in visual style and tone across all video content

### AI-Enhanced Workflows
- Use AI video tools (Runway, Pika, Synthesia) for rapid content generation
- Enhance footage with AI-powered effects, upscaling, and stabilization
- Generate B-roll and supplementary visuals using AI video tools
- Automate repetitive editing tasks with AI assistance
- Experiment with emerging AI video technologies
- Balance AI-generated content with traditional editing craft

### Motion Graphics & Animation
- Design and animate title cards, lower thirds, and graphic overlays
- Create motion graphics templates for consistent branding
- Develop animated infographics and data visualizations
- Produce animated Quranic verses and Islamic calligraphy elements
- Collaborate with Graphic Designer on visual assets for animation

### Content Collaboration
- Work closely with Content Strategist on video content strategy
- Collaborate with Graphic Designer on visual consistency
- Support Content Marketing Specialist with social video content
- Incorporate feedback from stakeholders efficiently
- Participate in creative brainstorming sessions

## Required Skills

### Technical Skills (Professional Tools)
- **DaVinci Resolve**: Expert-level proficiency in editing, Fusion (motion graphics), and Color Grading
- **Motion Graphics**: Advanced skills in Fusion, After Effects (optional), or similar tools
- **Audio Editing**: Sound design, mixing, noise reduction, music integration
- **Video Formats**: Understanding of codecs, resolutions, frame rates, aspect ratios
- **Export & Optimization**: Platform-specific export settings (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok)

### AI Video Tools (Required from Day One)
- **AI Video Generators**: Runway ML, Pika, Synthesia for AI-generated footage
- **AI Enhancement**: Topaz Video AI for upscaling and enhancement
- **AI Effects**: Familiarity with AI-powered plugins and effects
- **Prompt Engineering**: Crafting effective prompts for AI video generation
- **Hybrid Workflows**: Seamlessly blending AI and traditional editing

### Creative Skills
- **Visual Storytelling**: Pacing, rhythm, emotional impact
- **Color Theory**: Color grading for mood and consistency
- **Typography in Motion**: Kinetic typography and text animation
- **Composition**: Framing, visual balance, rule of thirds
- **Brand Awareness**: Maintaining ITQAN's visual identity

### Domain Knowledge
- Understanding of Islamic content sensibilities and restrictions
- Respect for Quranic verses and religious content handling
- Arabic typography and right-to-left text animation (preferred)
- Familiarity with Muslim audience preferences and cultural nuances

### Soft Skills
- **Creative Flair**: Innovative visual ideas and artistic sensibility
- **Attention to Detail**: Precision in timing, color, and audio sync
- **Efficiency**: Ability to work quickly without sacrificing quality
- **Collaboration**: Receptive to feedback and team input
- **Adaptability**: Comfortable with changing priorities and rapid iteration
