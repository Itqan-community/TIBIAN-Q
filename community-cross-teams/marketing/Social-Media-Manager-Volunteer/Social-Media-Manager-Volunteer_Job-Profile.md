# Social Media Manager Volunteer

## Role Overview
The Social Media Manager Volunteer builds and engages ITQAN's social media presence across platforms (Instagram, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Facebook, TikTok), creating content that inspires the Muslim community while growing awareness of Quranic technology products. This volunteer role offers hands-on experience managing social media for a mission-driven organization serving millions.

## Key Responsibilities

### Content Creation & Publishing (35% of time)
- Create engaging social media posts (graphics, videos, carousels, stories)
- Write compelling captions with Islamic authenticity and cultural sensitivity
- Design simple graphics using Canva or similar tools
- Schedule posts using social media management tools (Buffer, Hootsuite, Later)
- Maintain consistent posting schedule across platforms
- Create platform-specific content (Reels, TikToks, Tweets, LinkedIn posts)
- Curate Quranic verses, hadith, and inspirational Islamic content

### Community Engagement (25% of time)
- Respond to comments, messages, and mentions promptly
- Engage with Muslim community and followers authentically
- Monitor conversations about Quranic technology and ITQAN
- Build relationships with Islamic influencers and content creators
- Share user-generated content and testimonials
- Foster inclusive, supportive community atmosphere
- Address questions and concerns with respect and professionalism

### Social Media Strategy (20% of time)
- Develop monthly content calendars aligned with Islamic calendar and events
- Research trending topics in Muslim social media
- Identify optimal posting times and frequency per platform
- Coordinate social campaigns with product launches and initiatives
- Monitor competitor social media and industry trends
- Propose creative campaign ideas to grow following

### Analytics & Optimization (15% of time)
- Track social media metrics (reach, engagement, followers, clicks)
- Analyze which content performs best and why
- Generate monthly social media performance reports
- Test different content formats and posting strategies
- Optimize content based on audience engagement patterns
- Measure campaign effectiveness and ROI

### Collaboration & Coordination (5% of time)
- Coordinate with content team for blog posts and resources
- Work with designers for high-quality graphics and videos
- Align with product team on feature announcements
- Collaborate with community team on events and initiatives
- Share social insights with marketing team

## Required Skills

### Social Media Expertise
- **Platform Knowledge**: Deep understanding of Instagram, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Facebook, TikTok
- **Content Formats**: Knowledge of Reels, Stories, Carousels, Threads, TikToks
- **Algorithm Understanding**: How each platform's algorithm works
- **Best Practices**: Optimal posting times, hashtag strategies, engagement tactics
- **Trends**: Awareness of social media trends and viral content patterns

### Content Creation
- **Copywriting**: Engaging, concise social media copy
- **Visual Design**: Basic graphic design using Canva, Adobe Spark, or similar
- **Video Editing**: Simple video editing for Reels and TikToks (CapCut, InShot)
- **Photography**: Basic phone photography and editing
- **Storytelling**: Crafting narratives that resonate with Muslim audiences

### Technical Skills
- **Social Media Tools**: Buffer, Hootsuite, Later, or similar scheduling tools
- **Analytics**: Instagram Insights, Twitter Analytics, Facebook Analytics
- **Design Tools**: Canva, Adobe Creative Suite (basic)
- **Video Tools**: CapCut, InShot, or similar mobile editing apps
- **Hashtag Research**: Tools for finding relevant hashtags

### Domain Knowledge
- **Islamic Content**: Understanding of Quranic verses, hadith, Islamic values
- **Muslim Audiences**: Knowledge of what resonates with global Muslim community
- **Cultural Sensitivity**: Respect for diverse Muslim cultures and practices
- **Halal Marketing**: Ethical, authentic marketing aligned with Islamic principles

### Soft Skills
- Excellent written communication in English (Arabic is a plus)
- Creativity and ability to generate engaging content ideas
- Empathy and cultural sensitivity
- Responsiveness and community-oriented mindset
- Time management to maintain consistent posting schedule
- Adaptability to changing social media trends
