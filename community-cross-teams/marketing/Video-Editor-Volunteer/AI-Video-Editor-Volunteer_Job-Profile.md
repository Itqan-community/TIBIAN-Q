# Video Editor / Motion Designer Volunteer

## Role Overview
The Video Editor / Motion Designer Volunteer creates engaging video content and motion graphics for ITQAN's Quranic technology products using AI-enhanced editing tools. This role produces educational videos, product demos, social media content, and promotional materials that inspire millions of Muslims worldwide.

## Key Responsibilities

### Video Editing & Production (40% of time)
- Edit videos for YouTube, social media (Reels, TikToks, Shorts), and website
- Create product demo videos and feature explainers
- Edit educational Quranic content videos
- Cut and arrange footage with compelling storytelling
- Add music, sound effects, and audio mixing
- Color grade footage for professional look
- Export videos optimized for different platforms

### Motion Graphics & Animation (25% of time)
- Design animated text, titles, and lower thirds
- Create kinetic typography for Quranic verses and hadith
- Build animated infographics and data visualizations
- Design logo animations and brand elements
- Create transitions and visual effects
- Develop templates for recurring content types

### AI-Enhanced Production (15% of time)
- Use AI tools for video enhancement (Runway, Descript, etc.)
- Generate AI voiceovers for narration (ElevenLabs, etc.)
- Auto-generate captions and subtitles using AI
- Leverage AI for color grading and enhancement
- Experiment with AI video generation for b-roll
- Streamline editing workflows with AI automation

### Social Media Content (10% of time)
- Create short-form video content (Reels, TikToks, Shorts)
- Design vertical videos optimized for mobile
- Add trending audio and effects for social platforms
- Create video thumbnails and cover images
- Repurpose long-form content into clips

### Collaboration & Asset Management (10% of time)
- Coordinate with content team on video concepts
- Organize and maintain video asset library
- Document editing workflows and templates
- Share knowledge with team on video best practices
- Provide creative input on video strategies

## Required Skills

### Video Editing Software
- **Adobe Premiere Pro** or **Final Cut Pro** (primary editing)
- **Adobe After Effects** (motion graphics and animation)
- **DaVinci Resolve** (color grading)
- **CapCut** or **InShot** (social media editing - optional)

### Motion Graphics & Design
- **Animation Principles**: Timing, easing, keyframing
- **Typography**: Kinetic typography and text animation
- **Visual Effects**: Compositing, masking, tracking
- **Color Theory**: Color grading and correction
- **Graphic Design**: Basic design principles for motion

### Technical Skills
- **Video Formats**: Understanding of codecs, resolutions, aspect ratios
- **Audio Editing**: Basic audio mixing, noise reduction, EQ
- **Compression**: Optimizing video for web and mobile
- **Platform Specs**: Requirements for YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, etc.
- **AI Tools**: Familiarity with AI video/audio tools (Runway, Descript, etc.)

### Creative Skills
- **Storytelling**: Visual narrative through editing
- **Pacing & Rhythm**: Creating engaging flow
- **Music Selection**: Choosing appropriate background music
- **Islamic Aesthetics**: Visual style appropriate for Quranic content
- **Attention to Detail**: Precision in editing and graphics

### Domain Knowledge
- Understanding of Islamic content and cultural sensitivity
- Knowledge of Quranic recitation and Arabic typography
- Awareness of halal content guidelines (music, imagery)
- Respect for religious content presentation

### Soft Skills
- Creative and artistic sensibility
- Organized file and project management
- Ability to work with feedback and revisions
- Time management for meeting deadlines
- Collaborative team player
