# Content Marketing Specialist (AI-Optimized Distribution)

## Role Overview
The Content Marketing Specialist handles publishing, analytics, SEO, and audience engagement for ITQAN Community, ensuring reach and maximum impact of all content created by the team. This role uses AI-driven analytics tools (Metricool, Hootsuite, SEMrush) to optimize distribution, track performance, and grow ITQAN's presence across digital channels. The specialist focuses on getting the right content to the right audience at the right time, building community engagement and driving business results.

## Key Responsibilities

### Content Distribution & Publishing
- Publish and schedule content across all platforms (blog, social media, email, YouTube)
- Manage content calendar execution and timely publication
- Optimize posting times using AI analytics for maximum reach
- Coordinate content distribution across multiple channels
- Repurpose content for different platforms and formats
- Ensure brand consistency in all published content

### AI-Driven Analytics & Performance Tracking
- Use AI analytics tools (Metricool, Hootsuite Insights, SEMrush) to track content performance
- Monitor engagement metrics: views, shares, comments, watch time, conversions
- Generate performance reports and actionable insights
- A/B test content headlines, formats, and posting strategies
- Identify top-performing content and trends
- Make data-driven recommendations for content optimization

### SEO & Search Optimization
- Conduct keyword research using AI SEO tools (SEMrush, Ahrefs, Clearscope)
- Optimize content for search engines and discoverability
- Implement on-page SEO best practices
- Build backlinks and content partnerships
- Monitor search rankings and organic traffic
- Optimize for Arabic and English search queries

### Audience Engagement & Community Building
- Engage with audience comments, messages, and feedback across platforms
- Build relationships with Quran app users and Muslim community
- Share user stories and testimonials
- Foster community discussions around Quranic technology
- Respond to questions and support content-related inquiries
- Monitor brand mentions and engage with relevant conversations

### Email Marketing & Newsletters
- Manage email campaigns and newsletter distribution
- Segment audiences for targeted content delivery
- Track email performance metrics (open rates, click rates, conversions)
- Optimize email subject lines and content using A/B testing
- Build and maintain email subscriber list
- Automate email sequences using AI-powered tools
