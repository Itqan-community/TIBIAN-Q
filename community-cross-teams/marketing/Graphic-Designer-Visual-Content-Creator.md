# Graphic Designer / Visual Content Creator (AI-Native)

## Role Overview
The Graphic Designer / Visual Content Creator produces visuals and AI-generated assets for ITQAN Community across all digital channels. This role maintains brand identity, creates stunning graphics for social media, marketing campaigns, and product materials, while collaborating closely with the Video Editor on motion graphics. The designer leverages AI design tools (MidJourney, Stable Diffusion, Adobe Firefly) alongside traditional design skills to maximize creative output and efficiency.

**Mission**: Establish and maintain ITQAN's visual brand identity through innovative, AI-enhanced design that resonates with the Muslim community and showcases Quranic technology beautifully.

## Key Responsibilities

### Brand Identity & Visual Design
- Design and maintain ITQAN's visual brand identity system
- Create brand guidelines for colors, typography, imagery, and style
- Design logos, icons, and brand assets for ITQAN products
- Ensure visual consistency across all touchpoints and platforms
- Develop Islamic-themed visual language respecting cultural sensibilities
- Create templates for recurring content needs

### AI-Generated Visual Content
- Generate imagery using AI tools (MidJourney, Stable Diffusion, DALL-E, Adobe Firefly)
- Create custom illustrations and conceptual visuals with AI
- Produce variations and iterations quickly using AI generation
- Refine AI outputs with traditional design tools (Photoshop, Illustrator)
- Experiment with emerging AI design technologies
- Balance AI-generated and hand-crafted design elements

### Marketing & Social Media Graphics
- Design social media graphics for Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, TikTok
- Create marketing materials: banners, ads, promotional graphics
- Design email newsletter graphics and templates
- Produce infographics and data visualizations
- Design presentation decks and pitch materials
- Create print materials (when needed): posters, flyers, business cards

### Product & Digital Assets
- Design app store assets: icons, screenshots, feature graphics
- Create product marketing visuals and feature highlights
- Design website graphics, hero images, and landing page assets
- Produce user interface mockups and visual concepts
- Design badges, awards, and achievement graphics
- Create visual assets for product documentation

### Motion Graphics Collaboration
- Provide static assets for Video Editor to animate
- Collaborate on motion graphics style and design
- Create layered files optimized for animation (Photoshop, Illustrator)
- Design animated stickers, GIFs, and simple animations
- Ensure visual consistency between static and motion content
- Support Video Editor with typography and layout for videos

## Required Skills

### Technical Skills (Design Tools)
- **Adobe Creative Suite**: Expert in Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign
- **Figma**: UI/UX design, prototyping, collaboration
- **Typography**: Arabic and English type design and layout
- **Color Theory**: Color harmony, brand colors, accessibility
- **Layout & Composition**: Grid systems, visual hierarchy, balance
- **File Preparation**: Print-ready files, web optimization, export formats

### AI Design Tools (Required from Day One)
- **Image Generators**: MidJourney, Stable Diffusion, DALL-E 3, Adobe Firefly
- **AI Enhancement**: Upscaling, background removal, smart selections
- **Prompt Engineering**: Crafting effective prompts for desired visual outputs
- **AI Integration**: Seamlessly blending AI and traditional design workflows
- **Generative Fill**: Using AI for image extension, object removal, content-aware fill
- **Style Transfer**: Applying consistent brand style across AI-generated imagery

### Creative Skills
- **Visual Storytelling**: Communicating messages through imagery
- **Brand Thinking**: Understanding brand strategy and visual identity
- **Islamic Design**: Arabic calligraphy, geometric patterns, Islamic art principles
- **Illustration**: Custom illustrations and icon design
- **Photography**: Basic photo editing, retouching, composition
- **Trend Awareness**: Staying current with design trends

### Domain Knowledge
- Understanding of Islamic visual aesthetics and restrictions
- Sensitivity to religious imagery guidelines (avoiding inappropriate representations)
- Arabic typography and right-to-left design layout
- Muslim audience preferences and cultural nuances
- Quranic app design conventions and user expectations

### Soft Skills
- **Innovative Mindset**: Creative problem-solving and fresh ideas
- **Strong Portfolio**: Diverse work demonstrating range and quality
- **Collaboration**: Working with Video Editor, Content teams, Product teams
- **Detail-Oriented**: Pixel-perfect execution and quality standards
- **Adaptability**: Thriving with feedback and iterating quickly
- **Time Management**: Balancing multiple projects and deadlines
