# Content Strategist (AI-Driven)

## Role Overview
The Content Strategist owns the content vision for ITQAN Community, aligning content production with business goals and managing the day-to-day content operations. This role builds and orchestrates a content production machine using AI-driven workflows, leading a team of content creators, video editors, and designers to establish ITQAN as a leader in the content economy for Quranic technology.

**Mission**: Create a digital operating system for content creation that leverages advanced capital, digital capital, and computational capital investment to build a collaborative, non-siloed content team.

## Key Responsibilities

### Content Vision & Strategy
- Define and own the overall content vision aligned with ITQAN's business objectives
- Develop content strategy that positions ITQAN as thought leader in Quranic technology
- Create content frameworks and editorial guidelines
- Establish content pillars and themes rooted in Islamic values
- Build strategic partnerships with external content creators (sharing economy model)
- Define success metrics and KPIs for content performance

### AI Orchestration & Workflow Management
- Design and implement AI-driven content workflows using tools like ChatGPT, Jasper, Claude
- Optimize content production processes with AI automation
- Train team on AI content tools and best practices
- Establish quality standards for AI-generated vs. human-created content
- Manage content calendar and production schedules using AI planning tools
- Integrate AI tools across content creation, editing, and distribution pipeline

### Team Leadership & Management
- Lead content team including Video Editor, Graphic Designer, and Marketing Specialist
- Manage hiring, onboarding, and development of content team members
- Foster collaborative, ownership-driven team culture
- Coordinate with interns and part-time contributors
- Facilitate cross-functional collaboration with Product, Development, and Quranic Content teams
- Implement clear collaboration protocols for external partners

### Content Production Oversight
- Oversee all content formats: written, video, visual, social media
- Ensure brand consistency across all content touchpoints
- Review and approve content before publication
- Balance quality, speed, and cost in content production
- Manage content budget and resource allocation
- Drive continuous improvement in content quality and efficiency
