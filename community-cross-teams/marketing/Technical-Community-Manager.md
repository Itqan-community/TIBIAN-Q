# Technical Community Manager - Developer Community

## Role Overview
The Technical Community Manager is a technical entrepreneur who leads ITQAN's developer community through strategic orchestration, automated processes, and cross-functional leadership. This role combines engineering expertise with entrepreneurial execution, creating visual systems, managing community activation, and driving measurable growth through hands-on problem solving and strategic leadership.

**Mission**: Build and scale a thriving developer community around ITQAN's Quranic technology products through orchestration, automation, and technical credibility, creating bi-directional feedback loops between community and product teams.

## Key Responsibilities

### Community Orchestration & Strategic Leadership
- Lead community growth strategies and member activation initiatives
- Facilitate weekly board meetings presenting measurable activation progress
- Orchestrate cross-functional teams for community initiatives
- Drive momentum through hands-on problem solving and execution
- Establish and track community OKRs and north star metrics
- Become central force behind community management team execution
- Build bi-directional feedback loops between product team and community

### Content Strategy & Creation
- Curate team inputs into compelling articles with visual elements
- Write technical articles showcasing ITQAN products and developer use cases
- Recycle articles into social media carousels for multiple platforms
- Create quality hooks and lead magnets for community growth
- Manage community press and media content
- Integrate visuals and imagery into all content pieces
- Coordinate content creation across team members

### Webinar & Event Management
- Orchestrate monthly guest webinars with community calibers
- Identify and recruit engaging speakers on relevant topics
- Manage webinar promotion, registration, and follow-up
- Facilitate technical presentations and Q&A sessions
- Create post-webinar content and engagement opportunities
- Track webinar attendance and engagement metrics
- Build relationships with technical influencers and speakers

### Process Leadership & Agile Management
- Manage agile community development processes
- Align community initiatives with product team scrum processes
- Lead cross-functional coordination and communication
- Implement process improvements and efficiency gains
- Run sprint planning and retrospectives for community team
- Document processes with clear visual systems
- Drive accountability and deliverables across teams

### Visual Communication & Systems Design
- Create and illustrate complex systems for cross-team communication
- Design visual documentation of community processes and workflows
- Build Figma/Miro boards for strategic planning and alignment
- Visualize community metrics and data in appealing formats
- Create infographics and visual content for community education
- Maintain visual consistency across community assets
- Use visual tools to simplify complexity and drive clarity

### Automation Implementation
- Design and implement email automation workflows
- Create community engagement sequences and nurture campaigns
- Automate redundant operations (target 60% automation in first 90 days)
- Build automated onboarding flows for new community members
- Implement triggered communications based on user behavior
- Use marketing automation platforms (HubSpot, Marketo, Mailchimp)
- Measure and optimize automation performance

### Analytics & Metrics Tracking
- Calculate engagement, activation, and growth metrics manually
- Create metrics systems and data-driven approaches
- Present data in appealing visual formats for stakeholders
- Establish community OKRs aligned with business goals
- Track weekly progress against north star metrics
- Analyze community health and identify growth opportunities
- Report on community ROI and business impact

### Independent Execution & Deliverables
- Handle detailed tasks autonomously without micromanagement
- Deliver visualizable results that demonstrate progress
- Own end-to-end execution of community initiatives
- Drive projects from concept to completion independently
- Maintain high standards for quality and timeliness
- Adapt quickly to changing priorities and requirements
- Take ownership of problems and deliver solutions
