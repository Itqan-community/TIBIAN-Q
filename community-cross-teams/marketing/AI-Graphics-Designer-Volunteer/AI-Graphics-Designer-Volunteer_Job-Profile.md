# AI Graphics Designer Volunteer

## Role Overview
The AI Graphics Designer Volunteer creates stunning visual content for ITQAN's Quranic technology products using AI-powered design tools (MidJourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion, Canva AI, etc.). This role combines traditional graphic design skills with cutting-edge AI generation to produce high-quality visuals at scale while maintaining Islamic authenticity and brand consistency.

## Key Responsibilities

### AI-Powered Visual Design (35% of time)
- Generate graphics using AI tools (MidJourney, DALL-E 3, Stable Diffusion, Firefly)
- Create prompt templates for consistent brand-aligned AI outputs
- Refine and edit AI-generated images for final use
- Combine AI elements with traditional design in Photoshop/Illustrator
- Develop AI workflows that scale design production 10x
- Ensure AI visuals respect Islamic values (no inappropriate imagery)

### Brand Design & Visual Identity (25% of time)
- Maintain brand consistency across all visual materials
- Design social media graphics (posts, stories, covers, banners)
- Create marketing materials (flyers, infographics, presentations)
- Design product UI mockups and promotional assets
- Develop branded templates for recurring content
- Ensure Islamic aesthetic in typography and color choices

### Content Creation for Marketing (20% of time)
- Design social media content (Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter)
- Create carousel posts and educational infographics
- Design email newsletter graphics and headers
- Produce web graphics for blog posts and landing pages
- Design promotional materials for campaigns and launches
- Create thumbnail images for videos and content

### Collaboration & Asset Management (15% of time)
- Coordinate with marketing team on design needs
- Maintain design asset library and brand guidelines
- Create design templates for team to use
- Provide design feedback and creative direction
- Share AI prompting techniques with team

### Innovation & Experimentation (5% of time)
- Test new AI design tools and features
- Research AI design trends and capabilities
- Experiment with AI + traditional design workflows
- Propose creative visual concepts using AI
- Stay current with Islamic design trends
